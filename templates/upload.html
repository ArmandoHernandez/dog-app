Dog App!





This is my dog app!!


It scans a photo and detects on it either a dog or a human.
If it detects a dog it predicts its breed among 133 diferent breeds.
If it detects a human... it tells you his/her resemblance to a dog breed.

So.... upload your photo!










Any photo you upload will be used only to produce a prediction/resemblance.
After that your photo will be immediately deleted. No data of any form is kept by this application.
